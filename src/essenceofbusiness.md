Few points to remember always for decision making and challenging ideas/assumptions. 

1. The design should be easy for a person with ADHD. The target audience if general users. ADHD is a persona for whom we design and if this group is happy, our business will grow for the best experience. 
2. The unique value proposition is
OT2 is not a todolist.
OT2 is not a second brain dump.
OT2 is not a full fledged Project Management App. 

OT2 is a task organizer
OT2 is a task manager
OT2 is a productivity system
OT2 helps you think. 
OT2 is a structure second brain companion. 
AI is used in OT2. Not as Artificial Intelligence, but more of Assisted Intelligence. 
OT2 should score higher in Ease of Use scale  compared to Things 3 App, Any.do app and similar high ranked apps in the play store, App store, Web. 
The USP of OT2 is scoratic questioning to help qualify, clarify tasks. Thus the second brain dump gets organized, ready to be used when necessary. 

OT2 has simple task organizing features like "graph" where tasks are nodes and relating them gives meaningful connections and beautiful visualizations and "ripples" where recurring tasks get a seperate view gets created. Think of tasks as "what" to-do and ripples as "how" to do.
