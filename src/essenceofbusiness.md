## Business Model & Strategic Decisions

**Algai** (rhymes with Algae) — our mission connects productivity with marine ecosystem protection.

### Pricing: "Coral" Subscription Model

**Free Tier (Web/PWA):**
- 75 active pending tasks (forces completion, prevents hoarding)
- 3 AI Project Seeds (hook them on the magic)
- Browser push notifications only
- All core Freedom → Focus → Work → Review functionality

**Premium "Coral" Tier (~$6/month flat):**
- No discounts, no coupons — transparent "burger + coffee" pricing
- Unlimited tasks and AI Seeds
- Native apps (iOS/Android/Desktop)
- Guaranteed notifications: WhatsApp, SMS, Telegram, alarm-style wake-ups

### The "Algai Score" — Productivity-for-Planet

Quarterly productivity metric that triggers donations:
- **Commitment Ratio:** Completed / Planned tasks for the day
- **Socratic Alignment:** Time in Work Mode on high-impact tasks
- **Review Consistency:** Completing rating + reflection phase
- **Recurrence Streak:** 7-day streaks on positive behaviors

**Donation Trigger:** If user sustains baseline Algai Score (40% of Premium users), donate 1 month subscription (~$6) to marine ecology initiatives per quarter. User feels double guilt if they slack (fail themselves AND the ocean).

### User-Contributed Marketplace

- Creators export successful Task Graphs → publish as templates
- Standardized pricing tiers (Free / ~$5 Standard)
- **Stripe Connect:** Automatic 70/30 revenue split (creator/platform)
- Supabase RLS protects template payload until purchase

### Break-Even Analysis

- **Net profit per Premium user:** ~$3.69 after AI tokens, notifications, and donations
- **Break-even:** ~55 Premium users (at 5% conversion → ~1,100 active free users)
- **Viability:** Highly achievable with focused niche marketing

### Why This Model Works

1. **Generous free tier** builds sticky user base
2. **Flat honest pricing** creates trust (no discount games)
3. **Productivity-gated donations** align user success with planetary impact
4. **Creator marketplace** turns users into evangelists
5. **ADHD-first design** creates effortless UX for everyone
